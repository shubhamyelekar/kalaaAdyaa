Meet Your Teacher

Vaishnavi — Ashtanga Teacher Certified in Rishikesh

Vaishnavi teaches a breath-led, alignment-focused Ashtanga practice that honors classical sequencing while supporting modern students. Her teaching style is precise, compassionate, and deeply rooted in sustainable progress.

Whether you are starting your first sun salutation or refining a disciplined daily practice, each class is personalized to your level, mobility, and goals.
