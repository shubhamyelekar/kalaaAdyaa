Meet Your Teacher

Vaishnavi is an Ashtanga yoga teacher trained and certified in Rishikesh, India. Her classes are rooted in classical technique, mindful breathing, and steady progress for all levels.

She emphasizes safe movement, posture intelligence, and yoga as a lifelong practice—on and off the mat.

Why Ashtanga?

Breath + Movement

Each posture is linked with breath to cultivate focus, stamina, and nervous-system balance.

Consistent Progress

A structured sequence supports measurable growth in strength, mobility, and body awareness.

Meditative Discipline

Drishti (gaze), bandha (energetic locks), and rhythm help create a moving meditation.
